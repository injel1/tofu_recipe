CRISPY BREADED TOFU

A dish made by coating tofu in a crispy breading and frying it until golden brown and crunchy. The tofu itself is soft and tender on the inside, while the breading gives it a satisfying crunch on the outside.

Preparation Time: 15–20 minutes

Cooking Time: 15–20 minutes

Total Time: 30–40 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 block of firm tofu
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (panko breadcrumbs work best for extra crispiness)
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • Garlic powder (optional)
  • 1 cup cooking oil (for frying)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Prepare the Tofu: Drain the tofu and press it between paper towels to remove excess moisture. Cut the tofu into 1-inch squares or cubes.
  2. Season the Tofu: Sprinkle salt, pepper, and optional garlic powder evenly over the tofu pieces.
  3. Prepare the Coatings: Set up a coating station with three bowls:
    • Bowl 1: All-purpose flour
    • Bowl 2: Beaten egg
    • Bowl 3: Breadcrumbs mixed with a little salt and garlic powder (optional)
  4. Coat the Tofu: Lightly coat each tofu square in the flour, shaking off the excess. Dip the floured tofu into the beaten egg, ensuring it’s fully coated. Finally, coat it with breadcrumbs, pressing lightly to ensure the breadcrumbs stick well.
  5. Heat the Oil: Heat the cooking oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Test the oil by dropping in a breadcrumb—if it sizzles and floats, it’s ready.
  6. Fry the Tofu: Carefully place the breaded tofu into the hot oil. Fry in batches to avoid overcrowding. Cook each side for 2–3 minutes or until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Drain and Serve: Remove the tofu from the pan and place it on a plate lined with paper towels to absorb excess oil.
  8. Serve with Dipping Sauce: Serve the crispy tofu with a dipping sauce of your choice:
    • Spicy Vinegar
    • Soy Sauce with Calamansi
    • Sweet Chili Sauce
    • Creamy Mayo Sauce
